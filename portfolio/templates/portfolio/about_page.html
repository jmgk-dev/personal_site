Hi, I’m Jamie Kingett. I’m a web developer specialising in Python and Django.
I’m Jamie, a creative and enthusiastic web developer based in London. Working in independent record shops, producing/releasing my own music and then managing a series of iconic UK electronic music labels has helped me learn what’s worthwhile, what works and what doesn’t. I came to realise that the most valuable factor is creativity, and working as a developer gives me the tools I need to make ideas a reality. Recently I’ve been tailoring a Shopify store for a client and using Spotify’s API to retrieve and analyse data for another. I’ve worked on a series of freelance projects for clients including Sony Music, digital agency Hactar and independent UK music powerhouse Circus Records.
